Hi, I'm Itziar and I am passionate about Spanish. I come from the city of Pamplona in Spain where I gained experience in teaching both adults and children. I now live in beautiful Vancouver where I have been teaching Spanish to children in kindergarten all the way up to adults who have retired.

My Teaching Method

I customize lessons according to my student's personal needs. My objective is that my students start using Spanish from the first lesson. How do I do this? By determining my student's goals and assessing their level to design a personal study programme. The key to my student's success is good planning and communication.

As someone that has learnt both English and French as foreign languages, I understand your fears, hopes and frustrations. I will help you overcome these challenges.

Exam Preparation

I also offer customized exam preparation lessons for all levels. If you are looking to pass your Spanish Exam, I can help you to prepare.

Adults

Every individual has different needs and so I adapt lessons to your requirements. I offer beginner, intermediate and advanced classes and I encourage students who are ready, to take the DELE exam.

I also teach classes for those who want to learn Spanish for their travels. The emphasis is on combining grammar with speaking skills to develop basic conversational skills.

For more information, please make contact.

Teenagers

The teenage years are the perfect time to start learning Spanish because teenagers are able to progress faster than adults. I make my classes both instructive and fun by including visuals, music and games to help develop communication skills. Learning a foreign language can open new opportunities for study right up to university level and can be advantageous to a future career.

I encourage my teenage students to take the DELE exam if they are ready. Since Spanish is so widely spoken throughout the world, this knowledge will have much use outside of the classroom.

For more information, please make contact.

Children

Learning a new language at a young age not only improves confidence, it also leads to increased academic performance. This also applies to children who may seem too young to benefit from learning another language. To keep children interested, I make my lessons fun while still being informative.

I love teaching children my language. I teach to the following age groups:

Ages 5-7

An introduction to basic Spanish adapted to young children. Basic vocabulary and expressions are taught.

Ages 8-12

Reading and writing Spanish is introduced. In addition, vocabulary and expressions are taught.
